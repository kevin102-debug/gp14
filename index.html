The modern day GP14 is a two person competitive racing dinghy with spinnaker, constructed from either epoxy resin or wood. The fleet enjoys a competitive international, national and regional racing circuit for adult and youth sailors.

Every boat is built to order with custom fit-out options, including through-deck sheeting and Sapele decks. Spray-finished with only the best epoxy, varnish and paint, each boat is RCD-compliant. We use only FSC®-certified woods from sustainable sources—because performance should never compromise the planet.
