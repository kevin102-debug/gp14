Quick Reference Summary

Standard Sizes

  • 4mm: Most halyards & control lines
  • 6mm: Sheets (main, jib, spinnaker)
  • 3mm: Wire tails & small controls

Top Recommendations

  • Kingfisher Evolution Race for halyards
  • Gottifredi Maffioli Swiftcord for mainsheet
  • Evo Lite Race Taper for spinnaker sheet

Pro Tips

  • Mark lines with tape for quick identification
  • Add 5-10% extra length for splicing
  • Consider tapered sheets for less weight
